Welcome to AMBU

An Online Platform For Best Ambulances And Medical Facilities

We are building a platform, a website, and an application on which users can request an ambulance in less time . They can upload health-related documents to our cloud, donate or request blood on our portal, and order medicine in an emergency in a short period of time. On our app, we have also integrated a medicine reader software and report analyzer . This can suggest which medicines are best for you, and from our platform, you can also order those medicines quickly. As part of this platform, we have developed a portal through which patients can communicate with our doctors online.

Our main goal is to reduce the distance between the patient and the hospital by implementing our location-tracking system so that ambulances can reach their exact location in the shortest time possible. Through our navigation system, ambulances will choose the shortest path and the least congested route.
